💡 Key Insights
Data-driven observations from the assessment
📈Adoption rate is the single biggest driver of value. Northwind (80%) achieves ~950% ROI vs. Fabrikam (45%) barely breaking even at ~3% ROI.
💸Idle licenses represent a significant financial drag. ~1,930 unused seats translate to over $59K/month in wasted spend.
⚡Hours saved per user strongly amplifies ROI. Northwind's 6.2 hrs/month productivity impact compounds high adoption for outsized returns.
🚀Contoso generates strong value (~281% ROI) but has meaningful upside — 1,140 inactive users remain untapped.
⚠️ Risk Factors
Issues that could erode value realization
🔴Low adoption at Fabrikam (45%) indicates a potential failed rollout or lack of effective enablement programs.
🔴High unused license counts (especially Contoso & Fabrikam) create ongoing financial leakage without productivity gains.
🟡ROI sensitivity to 'hours saved' — if overstated, ROI could materially decline, especially for borderline cases like Fabrikam.
🟡Uneven value realization across companies suggests inconsistent governance, training, or use-case clarity.
✅ Recommendations
Prioritized action items to maximize Copilot ROI
🔁
Reclaim and reallocate unused licenses High Priority
Immediately target the 1,930 inactive users across all companies. For Fabrikam and Contoso, reclaim licenses or reassign to high-demand roles to reduce ~$59K/month in waste.
📚
Drive adoption in low-performing segments (Fabrikam) High Priority
Launch targeted enablement programs focused on daily workflows (Outlook, Teams, Excel). Pair training with manager accountability to increase adoption from 45% toward 65%+.
⭐
Scale success patterns from Northwind High Priority
Identify which use cases and training approaches led to 80% adoption and 6.2 hours saved/month. Replicate these playbooks across other organizations.
🔗
Increase depth of usage (app mix) Medium Priority
Encourage multi-app workflows (meetings → summaries → documents → analysis) to increase hours saved per user, especially at Fabrikam.
📊
Implement value tracking and governance Medium Priority
Track adoption, usage depth, and hours saved monthly. Tie business reviews to ROI metrics and enforce accountability at department level.
